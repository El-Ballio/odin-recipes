Haduken Chips!

Haduken Chips is Ryu's signiture dish!

Ingredients:

  • Haddock
  • Chips
  • Peas
  • Tartare Sauce
  • Lemon

Steps:

  1. Deep fry the haddock and the chips.
  2. Place the pea's on the hob and cook.
  3. Place cooked ingrediants on a plate.
  4. Add Tartare sauce and lemon for garnish.
